AMANA Integrity Gate

A payment integrity intelligence platform purpose-built for GCC teletherapy — designed to surface fraud risk, automate claim adjudication, and enforce regulatory compliance across DHA and HAAD payer frameworks in real time. Built independently as a product management proof-of-concept, demonstrating how a PM with deep healthcare AI background designs an end-to-end clinical fintech product.

Why This Exists

The Integrity Gate is the payment intelligence layer that AMANA Health — a GCC digital teletherapy startup concept — would need to operate at scale. Teletherapy in the UAE sits at a particularly complex intersection: high claim volume, variable clinical necessity standards across DHA and HAAD, evolving CPT code sets for mental health, and payer fraud risk that's materially different from in-person settings.

Rather than describing this in a slide deck, I built it. The dashboard reflects real clinical and operational requirements — ClaimIQ adjudication logic, provider watchlist management, real-time risk scoring, and audit trail generation aligned to GCC regulatory frameworks. It's the kind of product a PM with 11 years of healthcare AI experience designs differently from one without it.

🧠
ClaimIQ Adjudication Engine
AI-powered claim scoring with configurable payer rules, CPT code validation, and clinical necessity flagging — built to align with UAE insurer adjudication logic.
🛡️
Regulatory Alignment
DHA and HAAD compliance tracking with automated audit trail generation — designed so every claim decision is defensible and inspectable.
⚡
Real-Time Risk Intelligence
Provider watchlists, fraud leakage monitoring, and session anomaly detection — surfacing signals that static rule engines miss in high-volume teletherapy environments.
